Go Through Me Go Through You

100cm x varied lenghts.
pencil on cheese cloth
NP3:Research:Gallery, Groningen NL, 2025

The drawings are excuses for contact. The body, touching the fabric outlined by a pencil, is feeling its borders. Assuming itself as present.
When you touch the wall you are touching yourself first, and the wall second.
The place of contact, I believe to be one of contrast: where one is made aware of themselves through its difference with their counter-actant.
This is an in-between space, which holds the difference and exchange between what is out and what is in. Seen in passages that dictate change in environment.
go through me go through you is a series of drawings assuming the place of curtain, requiring interaction, touch, from who wishes to enter a new space.
